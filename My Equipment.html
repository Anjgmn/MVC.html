  • Technics SL1200 MK5

    • Classic turntable for DJs and music listners. Just running a turntable doesn't make any sound.
    For making hearable signal, it needs phono pre-amplifier, speakers like below :

  • Behlinger pp400

    • Compact-sized phono pre-amplifier. It amplifies the signal from cartridge and transfers it to speakers.

  • Presonus Eris E4

    • Active stereo speakers designed for monitoring. There are two speakers.
    First one is active, so it can control main power and volume.
    The other is passive, so it just receive the signal from the active one.

  • RCA to RCA cable

    • Line carrying the signal from amplifier to speakers.
